Welcome to the Euroboard.

A digital scoreboard that you can use at home as you watch the Eurovision Song Contest 2021.

Cheer on for your favourite entry using the Vote page for each event, and see what everyone things live on the Scoreboard.

For the best results, show the Scoreboard on a separate TV or monitor, and use your phone or tablet to Vote.

Toggle your device's “dark mode” to switch between a light and dark theme.
